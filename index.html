Why USM?

A universal, principle-based method for managing any service

Universal & Unified

Use it to manage any service. Allows workflows to cooperate harmoniously even in complex networks of service providers.

Principle-Based & Simple

Built on solid principles inspired by best practices, not prescriptive processes. Simple enough to learn and become self-sufficient.

Free & Open

No royalties to pay. Non-redundant to avoid duplication of work. Open to foster sharing of ideas and practices.

What You'll Learn

  • Core concepts: Services, Resources, and Workflows
  • The five USM processes: Agree, Change, Recover, Operate, Improve
  • Eight workflow paths through the processes
  • How to become customer-driven in service delivery
  • Service Level Agreements and Performance Measurement
  • Practical deployment with a USM coach
